Sinhala Grammatical Conversions

Sinhala Spelling correction and Grammatical Conversions for Students

Let SinhalaSyntax be your linguistic compass on the voyage of words.

Sinhala Spelling

Automatically identifies and rectifies spelling errors in Sinhala text, ensuring accurate and error-free content.

Gender Conversion

Transforms Sinhala nouns or pronouns to a different gender, allowing users to adapt the linguistic gender of words as needed.

Tense Conversion

Modifies the tense of Sinhala verbs, enabling users to express actions or events in different time frames.

Nominal Conversion

Changes the form of Sinhala words to nominal (noun) form, facilitating the conversion of verbs or adjectives into nouns.

Voice Output

Generates audible Sinhala speech from text input, allowing users to hear the content in natural speech, enhancing accessibility and communication.

Research Gap

What is Research Gap

While the available literature in this area is relatively new, studies have explored methods like rule-based systems, statistical approaches, and machine learning to enhance grammar accuracy. Notably, some projects focused on creating a Sinhala grammar correction service with formal contexts, but lacked voice input or output features.Comparatively, our proposed application addresses key parameters like usage for grades four and five, accessibility for all primary students, and activity analysis based on both text and voice detection. Our unique features make our application a valuable addition in the realm of educational tools.

Methodology

Our Methodology

The development of the Sinhala Grammar Conversion and Correction Application for Primary School Students involved the utilization of Natural Language Processing (NLP) capabilities. Within this section, we present the overarching methodology and intricate methodologies that will guide the development of this transformative educational tool.The program utilizes the capabilities of natural language processing (NLP) to comprehend and modify linguistic patterns, hence establishing an engaging and dynamic learning environment for young pupils to acquire knowledge of Sinhala grammar.

Main Objectives

Our Main Objectives

  • Function to detect and correct Sinhala spelling mistakes.
  • Function to identify the Tense in a Sinhala Sentence and convert it to a requested form.
  • Function to identify Gender in a Sinhala sentence and convert it.
  • Function to identify Nominal form in a Sinhala sentence and convert it.
  • Produce a Sinhala voice output of the result.
